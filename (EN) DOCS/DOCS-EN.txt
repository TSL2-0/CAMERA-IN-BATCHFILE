How to use this app:

Open a "CAMERA.html" in a chromium window.

Open the "camera.html" (This is how CAMERA.bat connects to the server)

Connect a camera and click on site settings in the html in chrome. Click on permissions and click "Allow camera"

Or you can wait until the packaged release is available.

Check **https://github.com/TSL2-0/CAMERA-IN-BATCHFILE/releases** for more info
